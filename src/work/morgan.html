About & Skills Section

Morgan Colton is a seasoned UI/UX Designer, as well as a former coworker and friend of mine. He came to me for help building his portfolio site that he designed, with the following criteria:

  • Single page website that emulated an application-like experience
  • Paralax scrolling effect
  • Links that scroll the user to the sections
  • Responsive for multiple screen sizes
  • Fast loading time and performant

To achieve these goals I kept the use of libraries to a minimum, enabled gzip compression on his server, and used AJAX for loading additional content. The initial payload for the site amounts to 635KB and load time for the site averages at just over 1 second.
