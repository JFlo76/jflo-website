Imprnt

Overview

Imprnt is a specialty screen printing shop located in the heart of Seattle, WA. I was contracted to redesign and develop their website with the following goals:

  • Improve SEO
  • Optimize for mobile
  • Make the site more user friendly
  • Provide users the ability to get a customized quote via an online tool

Role: UX/UI Design
Toolkit: Sketch, Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop

Identifying the problems...

The previous site had some experimental navigation that left visitors searching for the links, and the home page imagery only contained shots of the building and printing equipment. I recommended surfacing the navigation to the top of the page for better visibility and to reduce confusion as to which page the user currently was on, as well as adding images to showcase some recent work.

Analyzing the competition...

In order to make sure Imprnt was offering competitive services, I did a thorough search online to find a handful of print shops that had high-end websites.

These companies all have sites that utilize modern design layouts, elements, and functionality to provide an enjoyable experience and develop a sense of legitimacy and trust with the user (AKA the Aesthetic-Usability Effect).

The features I included for the comparisons were based on feedback I received from the client about some of his business pain points, as well as his wish list for improving the company's online presence.
